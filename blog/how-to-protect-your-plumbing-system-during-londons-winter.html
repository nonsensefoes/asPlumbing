How to Protect Your Plumbing System During London's Winter

Oct 12, 2023

TL;DR:
London's winter can be harsh on plumbing systems. Learn how to prevent frozen pipes, save on heating bills, and ensure a smooth flow all season long. 

Introduction 

Winter in London brings its own set of challenges for homeowners, especially when it comes to plumbing. Freezing temperatures can wreak havoc on pipes and systems. Here's a guide to winter-proofing your plumbing. 

The Threat of Frozen Pipes

  1. Expanding Water:
    As water freezes, it expands, putting pressure on pipes which can lead to bursts.
  2. Costly Repairs: 
    A burst pipe can lead to water damage, requiring expensive repairs.
  3. Disrupted Service: 
    Frozen pipes can halt the water supply.

Steps to Winter-Proof Your Plumbing

  1. Insulate Pipes:
    Especially those in unheated areas like basements, attics, and garages.
  2. Fix Leaks: 
    Even small leaks can let cold air in, increasing the risk of freezing.
  3. Drain Outdoor Faucets:
    Disconnect garden hoses and drain water from outdoor taps.
  4. Keep Heating On:
    Even when away, keep your home's heating on a low setting to prevent freezing.

Benefits of Winter-Proofing

  1. Cost Savings:
    Preventing issues can save on potential repair costs.
  2. Peace of Mind:
    No worries about water damage or disrupted service.
  3. Longevity: 
    Proper care can extend the life of your plumbing system.

Conclusion

While London's winter can be picturesque, it's essential to be proactive in caring for your home's plumbing. With the right steps, you can enjoy the season without plumbing woes.
